Where it Started

George Rylander is a Master Stylist...

George Rylander is a Master Stylist and Colorist with over 34 years of industry experience . The Hair Collective is located at 124 West 24th Street, Ste. 1. Trained by the award winning hairdresser, Frank Molinari, George went on to further develop himself as an expert hair colorist. He takes pride in his ability to masterfully cut and color all types of hair textures. His work has been seen on television, film, and the runways of New York Fashion Week.

Behind the Chair...

Behind the chair, George’s warmth and friendly approach will immediately put you at ease. You will feel right at home.Through his decades of experience both as a stylist and educator, he has developed an ability to create meaningful connections with each and every client. He takes the time to consult and collaborate with you. He listens and seeks to understand your concerns about your hair. With your wishes in mind, he offers design solutions and services that bring out the best in your hair, and in you.

As Master Hair Stylist...

As a Master Hair Stylist, George tailors his clients' looks to suit their individual hair textures, facial shapes, and lifestyles. His expert use of precision wet and dry cutting techniques enables him to produce modern and wearable looks that are edgy and fun while also practical and timeless. He specializes in fine and thinning hair. He diagnoses and cosmetically treats hair and scalp conditions, promoting the health and fullness of the hair. Product knowledge and education are key to George’s approach to healthy and beautiful locks. He loves teaching his clients how to style their own hair at home so they look like they walked out of the salon everyday.

As a Master Colorist...

As a Master Colorist, George has an uncanny eye for color formulation. He understands that beautiful and natural looking hair color complements your features and enhances your personal style. Some of his favorite looks include beautiful beachy blondes, honey-toned brondes, dimensional brunettes, and rich reds, with an occasional pink or purple for fun. He specializes in foil highlighting, balayage hair painting, and Color Correction. He is known for his commitment to maintaining the integrity and health of his clients’ hair. His attention to detail leaves his clients with results that look glossy and feel terrific. His aesthetic approach will leave you with a look that doesn’t just suit you – it will inspire you.

Empowering People...

George is passionate about empowering people. He is committed that his clients not only love their hair but also every aspect of their lives. As an Ordained Interfaith Minister for over 20 years, he relates to humanity as spiritually interconnected. It is his honor and privilege to support his community. He ministers many types of ceremonies and special occasions. He loves officiating weddings! Utilizing a “relationship mining” technique that he personally developed, he coaches and supports couples in creating extraordinary ceremonies that joyously launch their life long journey together. Also, as a second degree Reiki practitioner he is available to provide soothing and grounding energetic healing sessions upon request. George has over 25 years of experience as a coach in the personal development field. He is available for one-on-one and group coaching sessions that address many areas of living your best life, including finances, wellbeing, mindfulness, and relationships, to name a few.
